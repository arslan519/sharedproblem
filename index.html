✦ Our Story

Why we built
Shared Problem

Because the most isolating feeling in the world is thinking you're the only one going through something — when you're probably not.

The Problem We Noticed

Every day, millions of people deal with the same frustrations — a landlord who won't reply, a manager who takes credit, an anxious mind that won't let them sleep. But most people suffer in silence, assuming they're alone.

Reddit and Quora exist, but they're noisy and slow. You have to search, post, wait for replies, and wade through arguments. There was no simple, fast, emotionally satisfying way to answer the one question everyone really wants answered: is it just me?

What We Built

Shared Problem is the fastest way to find out you're not alone. Type any problem, see how many others share it, and discover what actually worked for them — all in under 60 seconds, completely anonymously.

No accounts. No judgment. No noise. Just honest, human connection around the problems we all quietly carry.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Short version: We collect almost nothing. We don't require accounts. We don't sell your data. Your problems are anonymous by default.

What We Collect

Shared Problem is designed to be used without creating an account. When you use the site, we may collect:

  • Problems and solutions you submit (stored anonymously — no name, email, or identity attached)
  • Me Too clicks you make (stored per browser using localStorage, not linked to any identity)
  • Basic analytics data such as page views and country (through anonymous aggregates only)

What We Don't Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identity
  • Your IP address linked to specific submissions
  • Any payment information (the site is free)
  • Any data from third parties or social media platforms

How We Use Data

The problems and solutions you submit are used solely to help other users feel less alone and find solutions. We do not use your submissions for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage only to remember which problems you have already clicked Me Too on — so we don't count you twice. This data never leaves your device and is not shared with us or any third party.

Third Party Services

We use Supabase to store submitted problems and solutions. Supabase processes data in compliance with GDPR. We use Google Fonts for typography. No advertising networks or tracking pixels are used on this site.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, there is no personal data to delete or export. If you have concerns about a specific submission, contact us and we will remove it promptly.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, use the Submit form on the homepage to reach us.
