✦ Our Story

Why we built
Shared Problem

Because the most isolating feeling in the world is thinking you're the only one going through something — when you're probably not.

The Problem We Noticed

Every day, millions of people deal with the same frustrations — a landlord who won't reply, a manager who takes credit, an anxious mind that won't let them sleep. But most people suffer in silence, assuming they're alone.

There was no simple, fast, emotionally satisfying way to answer the one question everyone really wants answered: is it just me?

What We Built

Shared Problem is the fastest way to find out you're not alone. Type any problem, see how many others share it, and discover what actually worked for them — completely anonymously, in under 60 seconds.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Short version: We collect almost nothing. No email required. Your problems are anonymous. Nicknames are stored only in your browser.

What We Collect

Shared Problem is designed to be used without creating an account. When you use the site, we may collect:

  • Problems and solutions you submit — stored with your chosen nickname and avatar only
  • Me Too clicks — stored per browser using localStorage
  • Nicknames and avatars — stored only in your browser's localStorage, never on our servers

What We Don't Collect

  • Your real name, email address, or any personal identity
  • Your IP address linked to specific submissions
  • Any payment information (the site is free)

Nicknames and Avatars

If you choose a nickname or upload a profile photo, these are stored in your browser's localStorage only. Your nickname appears on your submitted problems and solutions to give the community a human feel, but it is not linked to any real identity.

Third Party Services

We use Supabase to store submitted problems and solutions. We use Google Fonts for typography. No advertising networks or tracking pixels are used on this site.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, use the Submit form on the homepage to reach us.
