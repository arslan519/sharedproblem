About

Shared Problem helps you discover you're not alone — and find what actually worked for others.

✦ Our Story

Why we built
Shared Problem

Because the most isolating feeling is thinking you're the only one going through something — when you're probably not.

The Problem We Noticed

Every day, millions of people deal with the same frustrations — a landlord who won't reply, a manager who takes credit, an anxious mind that won't let them sleep. But most people suffer in silence.

There was no simple, fast, emotionally satisfying way to answer: is it just me?

What We Built

Shared Problem is the fastest way to find out you're not alone. Type any problem, see how many others share it, and discover what actually worked — anonymously, in under 60 seconds.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Short version: We collect almost nothing. No email required. Your problems are anonymous. Nicknames stored only in your browser.

What We Collect

  • Problems and solutions you submit — stored with your chosen nickname and avatar only
  • Me Too clicks — stored per browser using localStorage
  • Nicknames and avatars — stored only in your browser, never on our servers

What We Don't Collect

  • Your real name, email address, or any personal identity
  • Your IP address linked to specific submissions
  • Any payment information (the site is free)

Third Party Services

We use Supabase to store submitted problems and solutions. We use Google Fonts for typography. No advertising networks or tracking pixels are used.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the Submit form on the homepage to reach us.

Terms of Service

Last updated: January 2026

Short version: Be kind, be honest, don't abuse the platform.

What You Can Do

  • Submit real problems you or others genuinely face
  • Share solutions that have actually worked for you
  • Click Me Too on problems you relate to

What You Cannot Do

  • Submit false, misleading, or harmful content
  • Use the platform to promote products or spam
  • Attempt to scrape, hack, or disrupt the service

Disclaimer

Solutions are community-sourced and not professional advice. Always consult a qualified professional for medical, legal, or financial decisions.
